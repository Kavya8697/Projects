Who We Are

Rooted in 2000's by PANUGANTI'S Misty Splash is a family business passed down to younger generations located in Sarnia, Lambton County.
Misty Splash., Ltd is a player in the online market for small home appliances in Sarnia. Our first product debuted in 2001. It was quickly followed by the development of various types of humidifiers. As each began to thrive, we shifted our focus to creating an IoT platform.

Over 2 million active devices are connected to our proprietary app as of March 31, 2022. We provides a centralised smart home experience across three brands in an increasingly crowded market. Users can truly feel at home with their smart devices when everything is on one platform, leading to better lives.
